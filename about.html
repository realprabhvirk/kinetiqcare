About Us

Dedicated to empowering the independence of people with disability in Ipswich.

Our Philosophy

At KINETIQ Care, our motto is simple: Empowering Choices. We recognise that every individual has unique aspirations, preferences, and needs. Our goal is to provide the support that allows you to live life on your terms.

Based in Ipswich, QLD, we are an approved provider delivering high-quality, reliable, and compassionate care. From everyday household tasks to 24-hour high-intensity nursing, we bring professionalism and warmth to every interaction.

We are proud to work alongside our community, ensuring that our clients are respected, heard, and supported in reaching their goals safely and confidently.
